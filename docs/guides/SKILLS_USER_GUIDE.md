# Mandala Skills User Guide

Insighta Skills automatically generate actionable outputs from your collected knowledge. Access them from the **Skills** section in the sidebar, below the mandala minimap navigator.

---

## How Skills Work

1. Click a skill button in the sidebar
2. A **preview** panel appears showing what will be generated
3. Click **Run** to execute
4. View the result inline, copy it, or download as Markdown
5. All outputs are saved in your **History** (visible at the bottom of the Skills section)

---

## Available Skills

### Newsletter Draft

Generate a curated newsletter from your recent mandala cards.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What it does** | Selects the best recent cards (last 30 days), filters for quality and diversity, then compiles an email-ready newsletter |
| **Output** | HTML email sent to your account email |
| **Best for** | Weekly knowledge digests, sharing learnings with your team |

**How it curates:**
- Filters out low-quality or heavily biased cards
- Limits to 1 card per channel (ensures diversity)
- Ranks by quality score, selects top N cards

**Plan limits:**

| | Free | Pro / Lifetime |
|---|------|----------------|
| Monthly runs | 4 | Unlimited |
| Cards per newsletter | 3 | 5 |
| Summary style | One-liner | Structured |
| Custom template | No | Yes |

### Research Report

Generate a structured research report analyzing all knowledge in your mandala.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What it does** | Collects all cards (or selected sectors), groups by sector, then AI generates a comprehensive analysis report |
| **Output** | Markdown report (viewable inline, copyable, downloadable) + email delivery |
| **Best for** | Monthly progress reviews, preparing presentations, synthesizing research |

**Report structure:**
1. Executive Summary
2. Key Findings by Sector
3. Cross-Sector Insights
4. Recommendations
5. Conclusion

**Plan limits:**

| | Free | Pro | Lifetime |
|---|------|-----|----------|
| Monthly runs | 1 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Max cards analyzed | 50 | 200 | 500 |
| Analysis depth | Basic | Full | Full |

**Tip:** You can scope the report to specific sectors by selecting cells before running.

### Activity Alert

Get a trend analysis of how your mandala activity has changed over the past week.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What it does** | Compares your last 7 days of card activity against the previous 7 days, identifies growing/declining sectors, and suggests focus areas |
| **Output** | Trend summary (inline + email notification) |
| **Best for** | Staying aware of your knowledge-building momentum, spotting neglected topics |

**Alert includes:**
- Activity summary (what changed this week)
- Sector trends (growing, declining, stable)
- 2-3 suggested actions to focus on

**Plan limits:**

| | Free | Pro / Lifetime |
|---|------|----------------|
| Monthly runs | 20 | Unlimited |
| Notification channels | Email | Email + Push |

### AI Recommendations

Get AI-powered suggestions to fill knowledge gaps in your mandala.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What it does** | Analyzes your mandala's coverage per sector, identifies empty or thin areas, and recommends specific topics and resources to explore |
| **Output** | Recommendation list (viewable inline, copyable, downloadable) |
| **Best for** | Discovering blind spots, planning what to research next, balanced knowledge building |

**Recommendation includes:**
- Coverage summary (how complete is your mandala)
- Gap analysis (which sectors need attention)
- Specific topic suggestions with resource types (article, video, book, tool)

**Plan limits:**

| | Free | Pro / Lifetime |
|---|------|----------------|
| Daily recommendations | 3 | 10+ |
| Scope | Current sector | All mandalas |

## Viewing Past Outputs (History)

All skill outputs are automatically saved. You can find them in the **History** section at the bottom of the Skills panel.

- Click any past output to expand and view its content
- Use the **Copy** button to copy to clipboard
- Outputs are sorted by date (most recent first)
- Up to 10 recent outputs are displayed per mandala

---

## Tips

- **Add more cards first.** Skills work best with 10+ cards in your mandala. Empty mandalas will show "No content available."
- **Name your sectors.** AI uses your sector names (subjects) to provide more relevant analysis. Generic "Sector 1" names produce generic results.
- **Run Report after Alert.** Use Activity Alert to spot trends, then Research Report for a deep dive into the areas that changed.
- **Check recommendations regularly.** Run AI Recommendations weekly to discover new topics you might be missing.
- **Language detection.** All skills automatically detect the language of your card titles and generate output in the same language.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "No content available" | Add more cards to your mandala before running skills |
| Skill not appearing | Check your plan tier — some skills are tier-restricted |
| Email not received | Check your spam folder. Emails come from `noreply@insighta.one` |
| Generation takes too long | Complex mandalas (100+ cards) may take 30-90 seconds |
| Output quality is low | Add AI summaries to your cards first (they provide richer context for skills) |
